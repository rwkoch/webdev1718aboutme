I'm hoping to go to more countries in Europe and go somewhere in Asia or Latin America soon. I may also consider studying abroad when I'm in college.

Real-world connections:

  1. Travelling exposes you to different cultures, lifestyles, history, etc. outside of your home country. We're lucky to have such a massive and diverse planet to explore.
  2. Experiencing different political systems in action allows you to see their pros and cons first-hand. It can give you a new perspective on world politics.
  3. Travelling south over winter or spring break is a great way to escape harsh Chicago winters.
  4. Travelling with a group allows you to build new friendships and connections with people who are also interested in travel or a specific country.
  5. If you're looking to visit another country, there are a lot of helpful online resources to use. I recommend Google Flights for flight information and price tracking.
