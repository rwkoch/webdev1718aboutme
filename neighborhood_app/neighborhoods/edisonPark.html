Neighborhood Description

The Edison Park neighborhood is named after Thomas Alva Edison, who gave his blessing to this community namesake in 1890. According to the 2000 Census, its population is 11,259. Edison Park has one of the highest concentrations of Irish ancestry in Chicago, where they make up over three-fourths of the neighborhood's population. Edison park has the lowest violent crime rate of any Chicago neighborhood and is considered one of the best neighborhoods to live in.
