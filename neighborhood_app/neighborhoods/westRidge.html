Neighborhood Description

Immense diversity characterizes West Ridge, which features thriving Jewish, Middle Eastern and Southeast Asian commercial centers. The most famous is probably Devon Avenue, the heart of Chicago's Indian and Pakistani communities. People flock here to experience its many authentic restaurants, shops and ethnic grocery stores.
